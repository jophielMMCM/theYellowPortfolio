THE RIFT

2025

Short film - animation

A 3D-animated short film that chronicles a friendship between two girls that was torn apart by conflicting opinions on a major archeological discovery. It also serves as a narrative insight on the personal and sociological effects of low-quality educational materials.

While the plot itself was more message-driven compared to the rest of my narrative works and interests, given that an adjacent SDG was required, I still managed to play around with its' narrative structure by utilizing certain elements mainly inspired by documentaries and some short films I've previously watched.

This project showcased how I can extract opportunities from rigit, standard prompts.
