VOIDBALL

2025

Short film - animation

A showcase short film about a sentient green ball who runs from the depths of a polygonal void and down to the sky, where it achieves to fall freely — forever.

While the short film was made with the intent of demonstrating my skill in 3D Animation, there is still a deep thematic narrative that can be extracted from it. The ball jumps and rolls through various destroyed worlds and liminal spaces. But rather than lingering on those sights, the short instead focuses on expressing an energetic burst of vitality and inspiration in the face of such reality, proving that one can ultimately feel free.

As the grand finale of this exhibit, it embodies the ultimate thesis of my trajectory: a complete refusal to stay in one lane, celebrating a final, triumphant leap into limitless creative freedom.
