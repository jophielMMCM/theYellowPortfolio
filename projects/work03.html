GIZMO

2025

Short film - live action

A quick micro-short about a young man testing his newly-invented multiverse-hopping gizmo.

In the vein of "The Waiting Room," this micro-short was made in similar, if not more frantic circumstances. Against all odds and admittedly rough edges in the final output, me and Seth managed to create a tightly-knit science fiction narrative with a distinctly dreary mood within the course of an entire night.
