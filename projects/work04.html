PLETHORA

2026

Digital illustration - poster

The artwork is a dark reimagining of an older artwork that while still adjacent to some of my visual interests, no longer aligns with my current visual skills.

I explored how the act of creation exacts a burden on the creator's end, which directly reflected my state of having recently recovered from a major burnout at the time of its inception. By using this dark reimagining to push my technical skills forward, the piece can also be recontextualized as a thematic prologue to the following project based on the shared imagery.

Overall, this piece captures the exact moment of struggle required to peek out of the box of my personal and creative limitations. As well as how I can use stylistic imperfections as a the backbone for a beautiful work.
