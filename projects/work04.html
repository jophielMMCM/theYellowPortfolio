PLETHORA

2026

Digital illustration - poster

The artwork is a radical, dark reimagining of an older artwork that while still adjacent to some of my visual interests no longer alings with my current visual skills. It can also be recontextualized as a thematic prologue to the following project based on the imagery.

I explored how the act of creation demands a burden on the creator's end, which reflected my state of having recently recovered from a major burnout at the time of its' inception.
