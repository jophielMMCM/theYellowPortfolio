RUBICON

2025

Short film - animation

A short film about a girl who misses the bus home, which leads her to a reality-breaking journey through space and time — after an encounter with a creepy malevolent entity.

Made during an intense month of dedicated focus, "Rubicon" was an overall conceptual undertaking. For me, this was a personal narrative breakthrough as it managed to anchor a surreal, highly unconventional plot within a standard framework. There is no doubt that this project continues to heavily influence me, shaping my subsequent narrative skills and structural ideas.

It reinforces the exhibit's message by demonstrating a successful blend of contrasting emotional spaces — matching a chaotic, cosmic premise with disciplined pacing.
