RUBICON

A 2D-animated short film about a girl missing the bus home. After she encounters with a malevolent entity on the way, she unexpectedly goes on a reality-defying journey through space and time.

Project Details

Software: Animate, Krita, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, Audition, LMMS, Media Encoder

Role: Director, Concept Creator, Character Designer, Background Artist, Animator, Sound Mixer, Music Composer, Editor
