THE WAITING ROOM

2025

Short film - live action

A micro-short film about two strangers who meet at a mysterious facility, where they are instructed to wait for a long period of time. They get to know each other while an ominous fate looms.

Created under the tight constraints of academic instruction and a short production window, "The Waiting Room" represents a personal breakthrough for my filmmaking. Navigating the steep learning curve of horror and suspense, I managed to capture a sense of eerie starkness in the general atmosphere for both the submitted cut and the director's cut featured here.

This work marks my a major step-up towards expanding my creative range and honing my technical skills, while also crafting atmospheric world-building.
