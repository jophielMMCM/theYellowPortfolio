Curatorial Statement

The concept behind "The Yellow Portfolio" stemmed from a mental image I had long before I even brainstormed this project. There were times I imagined myself peeking out from a box, looking out to the world far ahead. In this exhibit, that box is conventionalism. The works of mine that I have curated are my acts of practice that when put together in a certain order, forms a path that lead to my gradual creative liberation.

Most of the works I have done so far has featured atmospheric, genre-defying worlds that blend the surreal with grounded reality. I have always seen mediums as interchangeable instruments that when done right, can serve a single, cohesive voice across works.

Statement

With all that's said, I did not make exhibition with validation in mind — showing-off what I created for the sake of rigid aesthetic consistency and academic completion. Rather, I aim to demonstrate what I can do with my existing works and making a thematic narrative from them, as well as the strategic creative versatility of one person, as both a multimedia student and an aspiring writer-director.
